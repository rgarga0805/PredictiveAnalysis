Working Paper File Coversheet (FAS)

Coversheet Guidelines

This form must be printed on 8 1/2 X14 (legal size) paper, completed and attached to the top of each volume of the Working Paper File. This Working Paper Files Cover Form must contain the signature of the deal team principal for the particular engagement. Each section of the Working Paper File volume must be separated by a single sheet of legal size paper and labeled appropriately on the bottom portion. These guidelines also apply to Working Paper Files comprised of multiple volumes. Each Working Paper File volume must contain a signed and completed cover form. All files must be drilled and secured with a rubber band. Completed Working Paper Files must be securely packed and sent to the Los Angeles Records Department. Any Working Paper Files that are sent in an incomplete form or contain discrepancies will result in an e-mail to the deal team indicating what is missing or what the discrepancy is, the Working Paper File will be marked incomplete and will appear on past due reports until the problem is rectified.

The document checklist set forth below is for guidance only. Different or additional procedures may be required depending on the circumstances of the engagement. Please contact the Records Department at ext. 5318 if you have any questions about whether a document or other material should be included in a Working Paper File, or with any other questions regarding the compilation of Working Paper Files.
